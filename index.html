There are a lot of things we should pack. But it's worth the preparation.

Two bikes (trail bikes preferably), bike locks, helmets, packed lunches, protein bars, two bottles of water per person, smartphones, emergency money, and a picnic spread.

On Friday night, we'll prepare the lunch.

Make some fresh hummus (I am much better at making it now), pack Bastourma, fresh ciabatta bread, and even some Arizona Teas.

This trip is very safe. And extremely scenic.

The 21 mile journey is a "recreational trail" meaning cars are far away, not anywhere near your daughter. The only thing to watch out for is the beautiful scenery.
