The trip was a success!

We biked and walked a total of 18 miles. We started the trip at 11:00 AM, and were picked up in Pacific Grove at 6:30 PM, meaning we traveled at a breakneck pace of 2.4 miles per hour (on average).

We packed quite a few things. But it was worth the preparation.

Two bikes (trail bikes preferably), bike locks, helmets, packed lunches, two bottles of water per person, smartphones (Glympse!), emergency money, and a picnic spread.

Friday afternoon, we prepared an amazing lunch.

We made some fresh hummus (I am much better at making it now), packed some fresh fruits and veggies, fresh pita bread, and even some Sobes.

We picked the perfect day. And it was extremely scenic.

The weather was ideal, about 72 degrees. We couldn't have asked for a better day for a bike ride along the coast.
